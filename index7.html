Shoe Stick

Shoe sticks are placed inside shoe to preserve its shape i.e. avoid folding of quarter (Back portion) and back stay parts of a shoe. They are made of 100% plastic with high bursting factor and GSM properties so they tend to have good strength, stiffness and non-flexible. We can provide customized shoe stick length according to client requirements. 23 cm, 20 cm, 18.5 cm, 17.5 cm, 16.5 cm, 15 cm etc.
